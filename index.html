Summing all areas, the total terrain occupied by phosphogypsum stacks covers 1120 ha = 2767.58 ac = 11200000 m2 = 11.2 km2.

As the Camp Nou stadium covers 58760 m2, the total terrain occupied by phosphogypsum stacks covers approximately 190 FC Barcelona home ones.
